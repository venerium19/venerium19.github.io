Welcome To Sarangani

Located in the southern tip of the Philippines, a tropical paradise, is the Province of Sarangani. Nature trippers find refuge in the clean peaceful white sand beach. Dive and snorkeling fanatics can’t get enough of the marvelous diving grounds where exotic and untouched coral gardens spread out.

Home of the 8-Division World Boxing-Champion

Sarangani, as a province, is more widely known because it is the hometown of the Filipino boxing icon Manny Pacquiao and is next to the Tuna Capital of the Philippines.

But little do people know that Sarangani province is named after its bay – Sarangani Bay.

Discover Hundred of Travel Destinations

The province is situated as the country’s major gateway to the Brunei-Indonesia-Malaysia-the Philippines - East Asean Growth Area. With its proximity to its regional counterparts, Sarangani is in a unique position to marshal its resources, entice tourists and investors, and compete in an international market under a borderless trade regime.

Governor

“
“Governor Rogelio Pacquiao, or “ Gov. Ruel” as his beloved constituents fondly call him, has always had a heart for the poor. Bereft of a comfortable life during his tender years, he is determined to create a better future for the next generation of Sarangans. Despite having been raised in abject poverty, he never felt incapable. Instead, he took it as a challenge to alter his situation, which progressed into a bigger aspiration: to change the lives of his fellowmen in similar situations. ”
