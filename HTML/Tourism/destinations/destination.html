Mesmerizing Beaches

Sarangani is a wonderful province at the southern tip of Mindanao in the Philippines. It’s known for its warm climate and laid-back vibe and boasts a dramatic coastline along the Sarangani Bay and the Celebes Sea.

This coastal destination is dotted with pristine beaches, including Gumasa Beach, Tango Beach, and Tampat Beach. Water sports enthusiasts will have a thrilling experience at White Water River Tubing. For a more relaxing activity, go for a nature hike and a picnic at Kalon Barak Skyline Ridge. The province also takes pride as the home of world-renowned boxing champion Manny Pacquiao.

Are you looking to indulge in an exciting beach adventure? There are plenty of beachfront accommodations to choose from around the area. Scroll down for our list of the top beach resorts in Sarangani, Philippines.
